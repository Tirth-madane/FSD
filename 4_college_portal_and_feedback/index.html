Admissions Timeline & Guidelines

To join the cohort of 2026, candidates must complete the following onboarding tasks:

  • Submit online registration form along with official high school transcripts.
  • Appear and clear the national/state Level Engineering Entrance Examination (AIT-CET).
  • Participate in the counseling round and register branch preferences.
  • Upload verification documents (Citizenship proof, transcripts, scorecards).
  • Pay initial seat confirmation fees to secure enrollment.
